Objective

Using a provided client brief, the goal of this project was to create a magzine that spoke to the identity of the given brand while also executing their unique goal outlined in the brief. My brand was Dr. Martens, and the goal was to connect Dr. Martens fans young and old, connected by the spirit of brand.

Approach

Dr. Martens is an iconic brand that values individualism. In order to maintain the free spirit of the brand while also communicating the goal of unity, I chose a punk revival design style. With the popping Dr. Martens yellow and the textured design elements, this design brings Dr. Martens to life./p1>
