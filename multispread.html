Objective

The goal of this project was to curate a multiple page booklet with three case studies of ads, showcasing them in a compelling way. A question that I have been asking myself as an Advertising student in 2023 is: how can brands ethically market their products and brands without promoting overconsumption?

Approach

I chose three campaigns that answered my question head-on. They recognized the climate crisis and took a constructive, responsible stance on the issue. As for the design, the color palette is a light organic combination of beige, orange, blue and green. I wanted to contrast the light color elements with a bold text to mimic the more urgent tone of these ads.
